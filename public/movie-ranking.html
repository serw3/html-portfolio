Serene's Movie List!

My Top 3 Movies of All Time :D

Spirited Away

This moview was one of my childhood favorites. I've watched it over ten times now, and the detail still amazes me!

American Psycho

Christian Bale is such a phenomenal actor, and the character and scenery portrayals were just so vivid and...psychotic. Despite the gory nature of the film, I still really enjoyed it.

The Boy and the Heron

This is a more recent watch, and it is supposedly Hayao Miyazaki's retirement film. That probably isn't true, but it was a beuatiful movie nonetheless. I wish I could have seen it in theaters.
